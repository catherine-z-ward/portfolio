Catherine Z Ward

International Photographer. Writer. Recording Engineer. Designer. Creative Technologist.

I study recorded music at NYU's Clive Davis Institute and spent 1 year in Shanghai studying interactive media, but most of what I've learned has come from the classroom of life, traveling around the world to document culture, religion, & landscapes. I'm interested in the places where sound meets image, where technology enables art, and where stories emerge from the spaces between cultures.
