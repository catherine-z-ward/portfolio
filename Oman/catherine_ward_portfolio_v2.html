Photographer. Audio Engineer. Creative Technologist.

I study creative technology and recorded music at NYU's Clive Davis Institute, but most of what I've learned has come from the classroom of life—spending a year in Shanghai, recording in studios across two continents, and traveling through 45 countries with my camera and curiosity. I'm interested in the places where sound meets image, where technology enables art, and where stories emerge from the spaces between cultures.
